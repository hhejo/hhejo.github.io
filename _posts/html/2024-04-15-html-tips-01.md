### 연락처 링크 만들기

`<a>` 태그에 `href` 속성을 활용

```html
<!-- Email link -->
<a href="mailto:name@example.com"> Send Email </a>

<!-- Phone call link -->
<a href="tel:+1234567890"> Call Us </a>

<!-- SMS link -->
<a href="sms:+1234567890"> Send SMS </a>
```
